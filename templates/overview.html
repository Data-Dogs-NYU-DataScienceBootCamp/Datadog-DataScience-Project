Project Overview

This prototype analyzes social media posts in two key ways:

  • Charts & Analysis – Dataset-level insights into platform use, country distribution, hashtag trends, and more.
  • Sentiment Analysis – Live mood detection and AI-powered text & caption generation.

About the Dataset

This dataset is a collection of social media posts sourced from Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. Here is the detailed metadata to allow for comprehensive analysis across platforms, countries, and timeframes.

🗣️ Total Unique Sentiments

191 distinct values categorized into Positive, Negative, and Neutral.

🌍 Countries Represented

33 different countries are featured in this dataset.

🏷️ Unique Hashtags

Over 975 hashtags were collected and analyzed.

👤 Unique Usernames

The dataset includes 685 distinct users.

📅 Temporal Coverage

Data spans Year, Month, Day, and Hour values for trend analysis.

📱 Platforms

Posts were gathered from Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook.
